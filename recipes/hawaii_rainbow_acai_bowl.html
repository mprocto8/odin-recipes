Hawaii Rainbow Acai Bowl



Description


Acai Blend Ingredients

  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 100-g (3.5 oz) pack frozen unsweetened acai berry pulp
  • 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk

Hawaii Rainbow Acai Bowl Topping

  • 1/4 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 kiwi, peeled and slice
  • 1/2 small banana, sliced
  • 1/4 cup peeled and chopped mango or pineapple
  • 1/4 cup strawberries, slice
  • 1/4 cup granola, optional
  • 1 drizzle of raw honey, optional but recommended

Steps

Prepare the Acai puree in a blender

  1. Add banana, acai pulp, blueberries, and a splash of milk.
    Tip: Add to blender in that order, as adding softer ingredients first makes it easier on your blades.
  2. Blend on low until larger chunks are broken down, then gradually increase speed adding a splash of milk at a time (Stopping to scrape down sides of blender if needed).
  3. When ingredients have mostly come together and all of the milk has been added, blend on high for 15 to 20 seconds more.
