Sautéed Peppers & Onions



Description

  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 bell peppers, sliced
  • 3 cups of sliced sweet onions
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large sauté pan or Dutch oven over medium heat
  2. Add peppers, onions and salt
  3. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and starting to brown.
    18 to 21 minutes
